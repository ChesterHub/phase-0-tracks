Elevator Algorithm
- When elevator door is opened
	-Until floor button is pressed, or until 20 seconds:
		-keep door open (with up or down selected)
	-If a floor button is pressed
		- Until 5 seconds have passed:
			- Keep door open
			- activate door obstacle sensor
	-Close the door
	-Travel to selected floor.
	- Open the door.

	- If no floor button is pressed:
		-Activate door obstacle sensor
		- Close elevator door
		- Reset Up or down selection
